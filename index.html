Our Services

Electrical Installation (Domestic, Residential & Industrial)

Our certified electricians handle all types of electrical projects with precision and safety:

  • Domestic & Residential Electrical Installation: Wiring new homes, upgrading electrical panels, ensuring safe and efficient power distribution.
  • Industrial Electrical Services: Robust electrical solutions for factories, warehouses, and commercial buildings, including high-voltage systems.
  • Electrical Repairs & Maintenance: Diagnosing and fixing electrical faults to ensure compliance with safety standards.

Smart Home Control System Installation & Maintenance

Upgrade your living or working space with our advanced smart home solutions:

  • Smart System Installation: Automated systems for lighting, security, climate control, and appliances, giving you full control via mobile apps or voice commands.
  • Online & Offline Smart Systems: Whether you need an internet-connected smart home or an offline system for security, we provide tailored solutions.
  • System Maintenance & Upgrades: We offer troubleshooting, software updates, and hardware repairs.

Solar Installation

We provide comprehensive solar energy solutions tailored to meet your energy needs. Our services include:

  • Full Solar Installation: We design and install high-quality solar power systems for homes, businesses, and industries. Our systems are efficient, durable, and optimized for maximum energy production.
  • Solar Maintenance & Repairs: Regular maintenance ensures your solar system operates at peak efficiency. We offer system inspections, panel cleaning, battery checks, and inverter servicing.
